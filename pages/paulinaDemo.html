Francisca Ferrada

International guitar player


I was born and raised in Chile, but I have travelled the world and the seven seas... everybody is looking for something.

And I am looking to fulfill my passions. One of them is traveling and knowing new cultures, food, people, and connect and collaborate with other musicians around the world.



That is why I have been invited to participate in concerts in the most amazing cities and towns in Europe, Asia, Latin America, Africa, North America.

Name the country, and for sure I have performed there playing with various bands in big or very small and intimate concerts.



The first concert I participated as a member of one of the most recognized Chilean rock bands is in this picture.

Thousands of people attended this concert in Santiago de Chile in 2010. It was one of the most amazing experiences of my career.
